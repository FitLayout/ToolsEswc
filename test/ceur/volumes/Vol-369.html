Citing the Proceedings

The proceedings are usually cited as follows: 

Christian Bizer, Tom Heath, Kingsley Idehen, Tim Berners-Lee (eds.): Proceedings of the Linked Data on the Web Workshop,
   Beijing, China, April 22, 2008, CEUR Workshop Proceedings, ISSN 1613-0073, online CEUR-WS.org/Vol-369/.

A paper in CEUR-WS.org should be cited using its online URL, for example 

Uldis Bojars, Alexandre Passant, Richard Cyganiak, John Breslin: Weaving SIOC into the Web of Linked Data.
   In: Proceedings of the Linked Data on the Web Workshop, Beijing, China, April 22, 2008, 
   CEUR Workshop Proceedings, ISSN 1613-0073, online CEUR-WS.org/Vol-369/paper01.pdf
